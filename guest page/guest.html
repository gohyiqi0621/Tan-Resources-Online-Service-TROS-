Frequently Asked Questions!

Find Answers to Your Questions

We’re here to help! Browse through our FAQs to find answers about our plumbing, electrical, and renovation services. If you need further assistance, feel free to contact us.

  • Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

    Yes, all our plumbers are fully licensed and insured to ensure the highest level of safety and professionalism. Our team undergoes regular training and follows industry standards to provide reliable, high-quality services. Whether it’s a small repair or a major installation, you can trust us to handle the job safely and efficiently.

  • How can I schedule a service with Tan Resources?

    Scheduling a service is simple! You can book an appointment through our website by selecting the service you need and choosing a convenient date and time. If you prefer, you can also call our customer support team, and we’ll be happy to assist you with scheduling and answer any additional questions you may have.

  • Do you provide emergency services?

    Yes! We understand that plumbing, electrical, and renovation emergencies can happen at any time. That’s why we offer emergency services for urgent situations. Whether it’s a burst pipe, power outage, or structural damage, our team is available to assist you as quickly as possible.
